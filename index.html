I have lived most of my life in Arizona. I love traveling through the United States, and have been to many of the western states. I am inspired whenever I come across something new. Being captivated by anything new is a blessing and a curse because I always find joy in seeing new things, but then spend most of my free time focusing on or learning that new thing.

My ultimate goal in life is to continually create. Whether it be new friendships, programs, art, or anything, I seek to grow and become a better person with better skills than I had the day before.
